Recruit loosely and grade on a curve.

The aim is to find people to recruit so they could participate in the testing of your web site. You’ll need to schedule appoints and get the chosen ones to attend the testing event.

The finds you are trying to make will answer the following questions

  • What kind of people should you testing with?
  • What is the number of people required during testing?
  • Where will you find the people, you are going to use for testing?
  • How do you make up for using their time?

What kind of people should you test with

Who you actually chose to test your site might not seem to be the easiest question to answer because of the fact below

  • Test representative will likely encounter the same problems as the people that are most likely to use your website.
  • People that are not part of you targeted audience might have problems that your current users might not.
  • People in your current audience poses domain knowledge, that others might not have.

Domain knowledge is the kind of knowledge needed in order to be able to use a tool or some form of equipment.

So, when doing this kind of usability testing you could start by loosely recruiting from a much more diverse audience and as time passes by you could only focus on your current audience.

What is the number of people required during testing?

It’s important to test as many users as you can, for the more users you test the less likely you will encounter new problems.

But with do it your self-testing you are not interested in how any problems you can find, but rather how many problems you can fix.

Hence settling with 3 users for each round of testing could be the only thing you need because;

  • They might encounter most of the major problems you site has
  • It’s easier to find 3 participants than a large number of participants
  • It’s important to do more rounds of testing than to have a single round while having a lot of users
  • Testing a lot of users comes with a huge number of overhead which could cause diminishing returns. This could money spent on food and time spent checking all mail generated.

Where will you find the people, you are going to use for testing?

You could consider to hire a recruiter, that will help you find people to carry out the test with.

 But this approach is costly an alternative is using any friends or family member you have to help with the testing process.

And if you are working in a company consider using some of you work colleges because they intend to make a good impression and have good domain knowledge, they will be happy to participate in the testing process.

How do you make up for using their time?

  • You can create some form of competition where people win prizes in the end
  • You could pay some of the organizers that where involved in setting up the arrangements for the testing of you web application.
  • Offer free food to those that come on time.
  • Provide gift cards to anyone who participates well.
