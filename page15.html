Some boring checklists

Checklist are used to keep track of the events that are going on during usability testing. This improves the likely hood you’ll complete all the required testing stages and scenarios.

Important checklist to consider

  • What will you be testing these could be the site, wireframes prototype
  • Send necessary emails to the participants informing them about the venues location.
  • What kind of users did you have, where they domain users or normal users
  • Remember to get feedback from all the users that where invited.
