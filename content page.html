Testing the web site

Your didn't see any elephants around

It is important to carry out usability testing after you have made your product, in this case your webpage.

A morning a month is all I ask

Given the fact that, the leading reason as to why people don’t do usability test is, they believe “it has to be a big production”, it’s complicated and will take a lot of time to complete.

What do you test when do you test

The testing process is usually made by the developers and they get to choose what will be tested each month.

Recruit loosely and grade on a curve.

Find people to recruit so they could participate in the testing of your web site. You’ll need to schedule appoints and get the chosen ones to attend the testing event.

Find some things for them to do.

You have to give people tasks that they can perform which will allow you to evaluate how well you have designed your website.

Some boring checklists

Checklist are used to keep track of the events that are going on during usability testing.

Mind ending made easy

While carrying out usability testing you could be inclined to play the role of a facilitator.
