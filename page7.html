Getting people on the right foot

Designing the home will allow your users to have an initial understanding of what the site could potentially be.

The home page should accommodate the following

  • Site Identity and mission: The home tells the user what the page is about and why the user needs to use this site specifically and not any other site.
  • Site Hierarchy: The home is also used to give an over view of what the site can offer both in terms of content and features and how it’s all organized. This is handled by the persistent navigation.
  • Search: Most sites can include a search box on their home page like Google
  • Tease: The home should also contain hints about the good stuff inside the webpage.
  • Content promos: These include the hot stories your site has to offer. Sometimes you can also include things like most popular pieces.
  • Timely Content: This is the content found on your webpage that needs to get updated quickly. And usually determines whether the user will come back to the site
